FAQs about Paellas

A paella is a Spanish typical dish rice-based. It is original from Valencia region, in the east side close to the Mediterranean sea. It is famous for its tasty flavor, and it can have different varieties including seafood, meat, and vegetables.

We think paella should be for everyone, from seafood lovers to carnivores without forgetting vegans. We have included different options within our delicious menu. Give it a try!

Our menus cost around 17 euros in most of our restaurants based in Ireland. Seafood and meat are regionally collected from experienced fishermen working in the Atlantic Ocean and from Irish farmers and butchers who have demonstrated care and exceptional food quality supply.
